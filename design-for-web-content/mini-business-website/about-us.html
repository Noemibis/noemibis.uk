About us

Mr Clark’s

Clark’s Healthy Food is a store leading health in London, It’s supplying its customers with a wide range of vitamins, minerals, health supplements, specialist foods and natural beauty products. With over 45 years of experience in the industry, our name is a familiar sight in London. Mr Clark’s opened this shop in April 1973 to give everybody possibility to eat healthily. Now, his sons are working with him to continue the tradition.

Why Healthy?

Eating healthy isn’t always easy, but committing to a healthy diet can be one of the smartest decisions you ever make. Why? Not only can eating well make you look and feel better, it can also save you money on future health costs.

Food Quality

A nutritious, balanced diet, along with physical activity and refraining from smoking, is the foundation of good health. Healthy eating includes consuming high-quality proteins, carbohydrates, heart-healthy fats, vitamins, minerals and water. Clark’s Healthy food will help you with that because we selected the best ingredients for our products and the best staff to do that. We have recently introduced our healthy experts training, to specialise in key areas such as sports nutrition, natural beauty, supplements or food. This is because we want to give you the best.

Mission

Clark’s Healthy Food is segmented into key areas: VHMS, Active Nutrition, Ethical Beauty, Specialist Food and Drink, FNSS. We sell a wide range of branded and own-label products across these categories and also own a number of trademarks for specific product lines.
