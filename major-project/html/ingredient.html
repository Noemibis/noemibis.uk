Miss Potato

Season
I have Central American origins and the best period to cultivate me is during the spring time.
You can find me in the greengrocer and supermarket from Agoust to October.
Benefit
Miss Potato along with its skin is one of a good source of antioxidant vitamin, vitamin-C. Regular consumption of foods rich in vitamin-C helps the human body develop resistance against infectious agents.
Curiosity
I'm not a vegetable, exactly I'm a humble tuber.
Fun facts
Miss Potato and her family is the fourth largest food crop in the world after rice, wheat, and corn.
