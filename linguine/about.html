Quick brief

Linguine teaches how to use commos sentences and sound like a native speaker.

The name "Linguine" has many origins:

  • It comes from Latin "Lingua" which means language.
  • The famous Italian pasta called "Linguine" has a connection as well, as a result of a common and international word understandable by anyone "Linguine".
  • "Linguine" is a feminine word in Italian/Spanish/French.

This website was created as part of the curriculum for the CodeFirst: Girls Beginners coding course. CodeFirst: Girls aims to get more women into tech by offering them subsidised teaching at a range of locations across the United Kingdom.

Code First: Girls is a not for profit social enterprise.
